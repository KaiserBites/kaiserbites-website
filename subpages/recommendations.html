Our Recommendations

From cozy eateries to hidden museums and charming bars – here's what we love in Vienna.

Restaurants

Gasthaus zur goldenen Gabel

A family-run spot serving up traditional Viennese dishes with a modern twist. Don’t miss the vegan Schnitzel!

Trude's Küche

Hearty portions, warm staff, and a garden that feels like a village square. Classic comfort food, done right.

Museums

Uhrenmuseum

Vienna's underrated Clock Museum hides hundreds of ticking treasures across centuries. Quiet and quirky!

Beisl-Kultur Galerie

A rotating exhibition space celebrating the culture of local taverns and everyday life in Vienna.

Bars

Der Späte Vogel

This cozy little bar serves craft beers and strong opinions. Friendly staff and frequent live music nights.

Sissi Spritz

Hidden just off the Ringstraße, this stylish spot mixes imperial vibes with creative cocktails.
